Common questions

How do I save my progress to another device?
Create an account in the app. Once your account is set up, your progress will sync so you can continue on another device.

How do I appear on the leaderboard?
Set a display name in the app. Your daily puzzle times will then appear on the global leaderboard.

What does the purchase include?
Letter Day is free to start. A one-time purchase unlocks all content, forever. There are no ads and no subscriptions.

I found a bug.
Please email us at support@letterday.ca or use the in-app Support option, and let us know what happened.
