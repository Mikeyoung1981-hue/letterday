Letter Day — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Letter Day ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using Letter Day, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the service.

Stored locally on your device:

  • Puzzle progress, completion times, streak data, and app settings.

Stored on our servers, only when you choose certain features:

  • Account information — if you create an account to save your progress across devices, we store your email address, a username, an account creation date, a member number, and a unique account identifier (UID). Account creation requires email verification.
  • Leaderboard entries — if you set a display name and submit a score, your completion time and display name are stored on our servers.
  • Feedback and bug reports — if you submit feedback in-app, the message and submission type are stored on our servers.

An account is optional. You can play daily puzzles and Adventure mode without one. An account is only required to save progress for use on another device, and a display name is only required to appear on the leaderboard.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Operate the game and save your progress between sessions.
  • Sync your progress across devices when you use an account.
  • Display leaderboard rankings for the daily puzzle.
  • Send account-related emails, such as email verification.
  • Respond to feedback and improve the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

4. Purchases

Letter Day offers a one-time in-app purchase. All purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect, see, or store any payment or credit card information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Local data is stored securely on your device using Godot's built-in save system. Server-side data — account information, leaderboard entries, and feedback — is stored using Google Firebase and Firestore, which employ industry-standard security practices. Passwords are managed securely by Firebase Authentication and are never stored by us in readable form. We retain leaderboard data indefinitely to preserve historical rankings, account data for as long as your account remains active, and feedback data for a reasonable period to allow review and response.

6. Third-Party Services

Letter Day uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — for account authentication and for storing account information, leaderboard scores, and feedback submissions. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Letter Day is a general-audience word puzzle app and is not directed at young children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data associated with your account, leaderboard submissions, or feedback by contacting us at the address below. Local device data can be cleared at any time through your device's app settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Letter Day after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data? Email us at support@letterday.ca.
